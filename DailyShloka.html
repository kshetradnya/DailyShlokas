Mission Group

Across the world, many people are losing touch with daily cultural and spiritual practice. This mission helps learners reconnect with timeless wisdom through one practical shlok each day, in their own language.

Our goal: make Bhagavad Gita learning simple, consistent, and accessible globally with guided meaning, action goals, and accountable streaks.
